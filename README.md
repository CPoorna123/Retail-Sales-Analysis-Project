# Retail-Sales-Analysis-Project

## 📊 Key Insights from Retail Sales Dashboard

### 1️⃣ Overall Performance
- The business generated **$1.06M in total sales**, with **$120K profit** and **2269 total orders**.
- A total of **17K quantity** of products were sold.

### 2️⃣ Monthly Sales Trend (Year-Month Analysis)
- Sales show **strong fluctuations** across months.
- Major peaks occurred around:
  - **2014-05, 2015-01, 2016-10, 2017-06, and 2017-11**
- Sales consistently increase during year-end months, indicating **seasonal demand**.

### 3️⃣ Sales by Sub-Category
Top-performing sub-categories:
- **Phones (~0.16M)**
- **Chairs (~0.12M)**
- **Machines (~0.10M)**  
These categories drive the highest revenue.

Lowest-performing sub-categories:
- **Binders, Art, Copiers, Bookcases, Appliances**  
These show very low contribution and should be reviewed for pricing/stock strategy.

### 4️⃣ Regional Performance
- **Western region** leads with **~0.33M sales**.
- **Eastern region** follows with **0.31M sales**.
- **Central (0.23M) and Southern (0.20M)** regions contribute less, showing potential for targeted marketing or supply-chain improvements.

### 5️⃣ Sales by Customer Segment
- **Consumer segment** is the strongest with **~539K sales (50.66%)**, almost half of total revenue.
- **Corporate segment** and **Home Office** contribute the rest.
- Indicates that business-to-consumer (B2C) is the primary revenue driver.

### 6️⃣ Product-wise Performance
Top-selling products include:
- **Cisco products (~23K)**
- **Canon products (~22K)**
- **Lexmark (~17K)**
- **Martin (~12K)**
These items should be prioritized for stock and marketing due to strong demand.

### 7️⃣ Profit by Sub-Category
- Some categories like **Tables (-9K)** and a few others show **negative profit**, meaning:
  - High discounts  
  - High cost  
  - Poor pricing strategy  
- Categories like **Phones, Chairs, and Storage** have positive profits, indicating healthy margins.

### 🎯 Overall Conclusion
- Strong sales from **Phones, Chairs, Western region, and Consumer segment**.
- Seasonal rises in sales during selected months.
- Some sub-categories are profitable, while others generate losses.
- Focusing on high-performing products and improving low-profit categories can increase revenue and profitability.
